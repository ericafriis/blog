Launched in 2005 as a graphic design studio, the Oh Joy brand now includes various licensed product lines, how-to lifestyle videos, and a daily blog with a focus on design, fashion, food, and joyful moments from everyday life.
Founder Joy Cho, has also authored three books and consulted for hundreds of creative businesses around the world. She has been a keynote speaker at Alt Summit, IDS West, Pinterest HQ, Target HQ, and more...Joy and her team work out of the Oh Joy studio located in Los Angeles.
The Oh Joy team includes: Joy Cho: Founder and Creative Director Jessica Hong: Crafting Assistant Courtney Ketchersid: Executive Assistant Angie Stalker: Graphic Designer Julia Wester: Creative Producer and Stylist ...as well as our photographer, Casey Brodley, our videographer, Jenner Brown, and all the other talented freelancers we are lucky to get to work with.
